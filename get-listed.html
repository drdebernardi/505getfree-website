Why Get Listed

Three reasons it matters.

— i.

Reach more survivors

When advocates search for services, your organization shows up alongside the details they need to make a warm handoff.

— ii.

Strengthen the network

Being listed helps other providers find and refer to you — building the web of care that catches people when they fall.

— iii.

It's free

Listings are always free. No fees, no subscriptions, no gatekeeping — just accurate information for people who need it.

Apply for Listing

Tell us about your organization.

Applications are reviewed within 1–2 weeks. Our team may follow up with questions or to verify services before listing.

Eligibility

Who can apply

Organizations serving trafficking survivors or at-risk populations in New Mexico — directly or through referral partnerships.

  • 501(c)(3) nonprofit or government agency
  • Tribal program or Indigenous-led org
  • Licensed healthcare or mental health provider
  • Legal aid or immigration-rights organization
  • Shelter, housing, or survivor-services provider
  • Faith-based organization offering survivor support

Not sure if you qualify? Email 505getfree@gmail.com.
