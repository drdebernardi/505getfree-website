About 505 GET FREE

Built by New Mexico,
for New Mexico.

A quiet, statewide thread of care — run by The Life Link, a Santa Fe–based nonprofit, since 2012.

Our Initiative

505 GET FREE is New Mexico's statewide hotline for human trafficking resources — a bridge between people experiencing trafficking (or at risk) and the local organizations equipped to help.

Established in 2012, the number is designed to be easy to remember, easy to share, and easy to trust: 505-GET-FREE · (505) 438-3733. One call. A real advocate. A referral to care that fits.

We don't replace the shelters, the clinics, the legal aid offices, or the tribal advocates already doing this work across the state — we help people find them. That's it. That's the whole promise.

Operated by

The Life Link

A nonprofit based in Santa Fe, New Mexico, providing behavioral health, housing, and anti-trafficking services since 1987.

Three Commitments

How we show up.

The shape of our work, reduced to its essentials. Everything we do fits inside these three verbs.

— i.

Connect

We link survivors, families, and concerned neighbors to the right local resource — the shelter that has beds tonight, the attorney who handles T-visas, the advocate who speaks their language.

— ii.

Protect

Trauma-informed, survivor-led, confidential by default. No reports made without consent except to prevent imminent harm. The caller's safety is the first and last consideration.

— iii.

Educate

We train healthcare workers, teachers, hospitality staff, law enforcement, and tribal liaisons to recognize trafficking and know what to do when they see it.

The Reality in New Mexico

Why this hotline exists.

New Mexico consistently ranks among the highest states for human trafficking per capita. Border geography, rural isolation, and poverty create conditions traffickers exploit. The 505 GET FREE hotline exists to interrupt that cycle.

— Fact 03

— Fact 04

Most victimsare U.S. citizens or legal residents — not foreign nationals.

The Team

Trained advocates on the other end of the line.

Every call to 505 GET FREE is answered by a human — a trained advocate coordinated through The Life Link's anti-trafficking program team, with specialized training in trauma-informed care, crisis response, and survivor-centered service.
