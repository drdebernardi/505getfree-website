A quiet promise

You don't have to explain everything.
You just have to reach out.

505 GET FREE is New Mexico's statewide resource hotline for people affected by human trafficking. Since 2012, we've quietly built a network of shelters, legal advocates, clinicians, and community providers ready to answer — in the way you need it, when you need it.
