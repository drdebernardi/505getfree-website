Our commitment

"You don't have to be certain something is wrong to call us."

505 GET FREE is New Mexico's only statewide, bilingual human trafficking resource hotline — available every day, at every hour. When you call or text, you reach a trained advocate, not a recording.

We connect you with shelters, legal aid, medical care, mental health support, immigration services, and long-term recovery programs. No judgment. No pressure. No police required.
